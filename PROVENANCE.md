# Provenance and Modification History

This repository, `caec-license`, is an independent licensing project maintained by Commons Against Empire and Capital.


## Provenance

This repository began as an adaptation of the structure, file layout, and publishing approach used in the Creative Commons legal tools ecosystem, including material derived from the `creativecommons/cc-legal-tools-data` repository.

The project draws on the general format and organizational model of the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International framework, including the distinction between a human-readable deed and a separately published legal code.

This repository is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or published by Creative Commons.


## Scope of Inheritance

Inherited or adapted elements may include:

- repository layout and publication approach
- naming and separation of deed and legal code documents
- structural conventions used in legal tool presentation
- adapted text where explicitly retained, revised, or replaced


## Nature of This Project

The Commons Against Empire and Capital License 1.0 International and CAEC0 1.0 Universal are distinct licensing instruments. They are not Creative Commons licenses, are not official variants of any Creative Commons license, and should not be described as such.

Any resemblance to Creative Commons legal tools reflects adaptation, reference, or structural inheritance only, and does not indicate compatibility, approval, or legal equivalence.


## Modification History


### Initial project formation

- Forked from the Creative Commons legal tools data model as a starting point for a new licensing project.
- Repository metadata adapted to identify this project as Commons Against Empire and Capital rather than Creative Commons.
- Project description revised to reflect an independent anti-imperial and anti-capital licensing effort.


### Deed and legal code split

- Established a public deed page as the human-readable summary of the license.
- Established a separate legal code page as the operative legal text.
- Clarified that the deed is explanatory only and that the legal code controls in the event of conflict.


### CAEC-specific adaptation

- Replaced Creative Commons naming with Commons Against Empire and Capital naming.
- Replaced Creative Commons institutional disclaimers with Commons Against Empire and Capital disclaimers.
- Began adapting license-family terminology to reflect CAEC-specific instruments, including:
  - Commons Against Empire and Capital License 1.0 International
  - CAEC0 1.0 Universal

### Documentation intent

- This file exists to preserve visible lineage and document the project's relationship to upstream source material.
- Additional edits, redlines, and drafting notes may be recorded here over time.


## Attribution and Notice

Where upstream material has been adapted, this repository aims to preserve clear notice of that heritage while identifying all resulting CAEC instruments as distinct works.

Users and readers should evaluate the text of each CAEC instrument on its own terms, not as a Creative Commons license or as a modified Creative Commons license carrying Creative Commons endorsement.

This project is under active development. The repository may contain draft language, adapted language, and original language in varying stages of revision.
